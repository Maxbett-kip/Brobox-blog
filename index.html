🎤 Brobox: The Kalenjin MC Bringing Laughter, Culture, and Energy to Kenyan Stages

Meta Description: Discover Brobox, the rising Kalenjin MC and comedian who’s lighting up Kenya’s entertainment scene with high-energy performances, cultural pride, and viral comedy moments.

🎧 Who Is Brobox?

Brobox, born Evans Cheruiyot, is a Kenyan Master of Ceremonies and comedian known for blending Kalenjin humor and vibrant energy. From university events to national media buzz, he’s become a rising voice in Kenya’s cultural scene.

🎓 From Campus to Crowd Favorite

While at Egerton University, Brobox quickly gained recognition as a campus MC. In 2016, he ran for Head of Entertainment, Sports, and Culture — highlighting his early leadership and entertainment chops. Though he didn’t win, his passion only grew stronger.

📞 A Bold Call to the Deputy President

In 2018, Brobox made headlines when he called then–Deputy President William Ruto to invite him to his comedy show. It was a daring, viral moment that underscored his confidence and ambition.

🎶 The Brobox & DJ Sutai Collab

Brobox’s high-energy shout-outs and comic timing blend perfectly with DJ Sutai’s party mixes. Their work is a staple at weddings, rural events, and in matatus across the Rift Valley.

🌍 Why Brobox Matters

Brobox doesn’t try to blend into Nairobi’s mainstream — he brings his rural roots to the spotlight. By championing Kalenjin language and culture in his performances, he’s amplifying a community’s voice in a national space.

📢 Final Word: A Local Legend in the Making

Brobox is more than an MC — he’s a cultural ambassador. With his humor, charm, and unapologetic love for Kalenjin identity, he’s helping reshape the Kenyan entertainment space, one laugh and shout-out at a time.
